# Bharat-Sahayak-AI
AI-powered government scheme eligibility and recommendation platform for Indian citizens.

# Problem:
Millions of Indian citizens miss out on government welfare schemes because information is scattered across multiple websites, eligibility criteria are difficult to understand, and many people are unaware of the benefits available to them.

# Solution:
BharatSahayak AI is an AI-powered platform that helps citizens discover government schemes they are eligible for. Users enter basic details such as age, income, occupation, gender, and state. The system automatically matches them with relevant schemes and explains benefits, eligibility requirements, and application procedures in simple language.

# Key Features:

Automated scheme eligibility checking
AI-powered multilingual assistance
Voice-based interaction for accessibility
Personalized scheme recommendations
PDF eligibility certificates/reports
Proactive notifications for newly available schemes
User-friendly web interface

# Impact:
The platform reduces the gap between citizens and government welfare programs by making benefit discovery simple, accessible, and personalized. Instead of citizens searching for schemes, the system helps the right schemes reach the right people.
